ABOUT

HyperBazaar is a departmental store selling cosmetics, ladies fabrics, artificial jewellery, shoes, ladies bags, watches,men shirt,t.shirt,jeans,coat,kids clothes and more at wholesale rates in a comfortable retail environment. Open 7 days a week throughout the year, we offer an unprecedented level of service with maximum assortment of products under one roof.

We have introduced a new concept whereby all middle men have been eliminated from the supply chain so as to minimize costs and transfer the resulting cost benefits to the customers in the form of reduced prices. This efficient business model coupled with low margins has made it possible for Hyper Bazar to sell its products at the most competitive prices in Pakistan.
